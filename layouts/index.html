Help

Frequently asked questions

What is an alias??

An alias is a proxy email address: all emails sent to this email address will be forwarded to your personal email address.

Do you offer discounts?

We offer free premium account for education (student, professor or technical staff working at an educational institude). Just send us an email at hi@simplelogin with your student ID or certificate.

Is email alias permanent?

Yes! An email alias is actually a normal email address that exists forever unless you remove it on your SimpleLogin dashboard.

What is your refund policy?

We offer 7-days money back guarantee, you do not need a reason but we appreciate your feedback.

What happens when I reach the maximum number of alias?

When you click on Sign In With SimpleLogin, an alias will be picked randomly from the alias you have created.

In all the cases, your personal email is not communicated to the website.

How secure is SimpleLogin?

Protecting your data is our raison d’être. SimpleLogin implements all the best practices in terms of data security.

As a French company, SimpleLogin is GDPR-compliant.
